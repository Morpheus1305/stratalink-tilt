# Update Hero Page Asset Coverage Display

## Context
The landing page "TILT - LIVE FEED" panel currently shows 5 assets:
BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP, BNB. This needs to reflect the expanded asset
universe and show the institutional and RWA tokens that ADGM cares
about. The "LIVE - 26 VENUES" badge is correct and should remain.

## Changes

### 1. Expand the Asset Coverage list

Replace the current 5-token list with 8-10 tokens that demonstrate
breadth across all asset categories. Show the full list with a scroll
or "view all" affordance for the remaining tokens.

**Display these tokens in this order:**

| Token | Name | Category |
|-------|------|----------|
| BTC | Bitcoin | Reserve Asset |
| ETH | Ethereum | Reserve Asset |
| SOL | Solana | High-Volume Liquidity |
| XRP | Ripple | High-Volume Liquidity |
| LINK | Chainlink | Financial Infrastructure |
| PAXG | Paxos Gold | Digital Securities & RWA |
| BUIDL | BlackRock BUIDL | Digital Securities & RWA |
| ONDO | Ondo Finance | Digital Securities & RWA |
| USDC | USD Coin | Stablecoin Infrastructure |
| USDT | Tether | Stablecoin Infrastructure |

**Why this order:**
- Opens with BTC/ETH (the reserve assets everyone recognises)
- Adds SOL and XRP (high-volume institutional tokens)
- LINK (DeFi infrastructure, institutional grade)
- PAXG (tokenized gold - immediately recognisable as a traditional
  asset class in digital form)
- BUIDL (BlackRock's tokenized Treasury fund - the single most
  powerful institutional signal on the platform)
- ONDO (leading RWA infrastructure token)
- USDC/USDT (stablecoin infrastructure - ADGM's FRT category)

This list tells a story: reserve assets, liquid tokens, DeFi
infrastructure, tokenized commodities, tokenized securities,
stablecoins. It demonstrates that TILT is an institutional platform,
not a crypto dashboard.

### 3. Keep remaining tokens accessible

Below the visible list, add a subtle "View all 25 assets" link or
a scroll indicator showing there are more tokens. The hero page
should showcase the headline tokens, not list all 25.

### 4. Update the Global PoLi score context

The GLOBAL POLI ring currently shows 43 CCC. This appears to be the
BTC-specific score, not a true global composite. If a global composite
across all assets exists, use that. If not, label it clearly as
"BTC PoLi" rather than "GLOBAL POLI" to avoid confusion.

## What NOT to Change
- The "LIVE - 26 VENUES" badge - correct and should remain
- The AGGREGATED DEPTH panel - keep as is
- The overall dark styling and layout - keep as is
- The TILT - LIVE FEED header - keep as is
