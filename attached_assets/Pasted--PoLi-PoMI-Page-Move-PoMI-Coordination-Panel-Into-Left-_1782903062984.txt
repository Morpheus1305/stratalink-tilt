## Priority
DEMO-DAY FIX — needed before the 2 July ADGM pilot call.
Incremental change only — do not re-run any earlier prompts.

## Context
The Cross-Venue Liquidity Stability panel currently has a large empty space
below the Cross-Margin Contagion Risk box (bottom-left of the page). The
PoMI — Market Integrity Coordination panel is currently sitting above the
EFI / FRBD charts, separate from the rest of the PoMI content.

The October 2025 reference layout shows the PoMI coordination panel tucked
at the bottom of the left column, below the heatmap area — which is exactly
where the empty space currently is. This change fills the gap, matches the
reference, and groups all PoMI content together on the left side of the page.

Move the PoMI — Market Integrity Coordination section out of its current
position (above the EFI / FRBD charts) and place it inside the

Contagion Risk box.

## Styling — PoMI Coordination Section (match October 2025 reference)

The October 2025 reference shows the coordination panel as a compact
three-column row:

- Each of the three sub-indicators (Threshold Definition, Throttle
  Activation, Venue Synchronisation) occupies one third of the width
- Status label in the appropriate colour:
    AMBER (#FFB300) for WEAKENING, DELAYED, MINOR LATENCY, MINOR DRIFT
    RED (#FF5252) for TRIGGERED, BREACHED, FRAGMENTING
    GREEN (#00E676) for COORDINATED, STANDBY (at full capacity), STABLE
- Numeric value right-aligned in the same colour as the status label
- Progress bar beneath the label, same colour, proportional to the value
  (0–100 scale)
- Section background: same dark panel background as the rest of the left
  column (#0F151F)
- No additional border or card wrapper — flows naturally as part of the
  containing panel

The current live values (WEAKENING 75, STANDBY 91, MINOR DRIFT 65) are
live data and will differ from the October 2025 reference values
(WEAKENING 76, DELAYED 69, MINOR LATENCY 67). That is correct and
expected — values are live, layout and styling match the reference.
