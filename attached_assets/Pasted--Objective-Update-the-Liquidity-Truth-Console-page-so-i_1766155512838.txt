## Objective
Update the Liquidity Truth Console page so it clearly answers the question:

**“Why price tells you almost nothing about how tradable an asset really is.”**

This is a **UI/UX positioning change only**.  
Do NOT change any data fetching, routing, or LIS logic.

---

## 1. Add a positioning line at the top of the Liquidity Truth Console

At the top of the main Liquidity Truth Console page (above the main metrics/cards), add the following one-sentence positioning line:

> **“Price tells you where the market traded — liquidity quality tells you whether you *could* trade.”**

### Styling guidance
- Render this as a short headline or callout
- Slightly muted but prominent (e.g. medium font weight, subtle emphasis)
- It should clearly frame how the user interprets the data below

---

## 2. De-emphasize price as a primary signal

Wherever the current mid price / price display appears in the Liquidity Truth Console:

- Reduce its visual dominance:
  - Smaller font size
  - Muted or secondary color
- Treat price as **context**, not the main insight

Example intent (do not hardcode text):
- Price should read like a reference value, not the headline metric

---

## 3. Elevate liquidity depth bands as the primary insight

Without changing any data or calculations:

- Visually emphasize depth bands, especially:
  - **25 bps**
  - **50 bps**
- These should feel like the *answer* to “how tradable is this asset?”

Guidance:
- Depth notional values should be easier to scan than price
- Consider grouping them under a label such as:
  - “Liquidity Quality”
  - or “Executable Depth”

---

## 4. Do NOT change any of the following

- Do NOT modify API calls
- Do NOT modify LIS, adapters, or routing
- Do NOT add new metrics
- Do NOT remove existing metrics
- Do NOT change venue or symbol logic

This is a **pure presentation and framing update**.

---

## Success criteria

After the change:
- A user should immediately understand that **tradability ≠ price**
- The page should naturally answer:
  > “How much can I trade without moving the market?”
- Price is still visible, but no longer the focal point
- Liquidity depth visually leads the interpretation

---

## Context

This console is meant to communicate **liquidity truth**, not price speculation.  
The goal is to make liquidity quality the primary lens through which all assets are viewed.
